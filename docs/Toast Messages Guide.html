06

Do & don't

Quick guardrails so toasts stay calm, helpful, and on-brand.

Do · short, factual title

"Booking confirmed", "Payment received", "Stock low". Verb + subject. The detail belongs in the body.

Don't · vague or apologetic

"Sorry, something went wrong" tells the user nothing. Be specific: "Xero push failed — connection timed out."

Do · one primary action max

"Retry now" / "Open log" / "Undo". One emphasized action, one ghost secondary. Anything more belongs in a dialog.

Don't · stack tones randomly

Avoid showing three different tones at once. If everything looks urgent, nothing is.

Do · auto-dismiss the calm tones

Success and Info disappear after 4s. Warning lingers if there's an action. Error stays until acknowledged.

Don't · block the canvas

A toast should never cover what the user is doing. Use a dialog for blocking decisions, a banner for persistent state.
